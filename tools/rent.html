Rent Fairness (Vancouver Zones)

Benchmark your rent against the latest CMHC Rental Market Survey for your CMHC Zone and bedroom type (purpose‑built rentals).

Source: CMHC Housing Market Information Portal (zone‑level medians/averages and reliability letters), annual Rental Market Survey tables. Purpose‑built market only; inclusions vary. Guidance only.

About “Reliability”

CMHC assigns a letter to indicate the reliability of each estimate in the Rental Market Survey:

  • a – Excellent
  • b – Very good
  • c – Good
  • d – Poor (use with caution)

You’ll see this letter next to the benchmark in your result. It reflects sample size and data quality and is published with the HMIP zone tables for Vancouver. [1](https://esricanada.maps.arcgis.com/home/item.html?id=b9756a12307d43a9a12cf25b92d3b83a)
