Rent Fairness (Canada‑wide)

Benchmark your rent against the latest CMHC Rental Market Survey (RMS) for your City and Zone/Neighbourhood, by bedroom type (purpose‑built rentals).

About “Reliability”

CMHC assigns a letter to indicate reliability for each RMS estimate:

  • a – Excellent
  • b – Very good
  • c – Good
  • d – Poor (use with caution)

You’ll see this letter next to the benchmark in your result. It reflects sample size and data quality and is published with CMHC HMIP zone tables.

Source: CMHC Housing Market Information Portal & Rental Market Survey data tables (annual). Purpose‑built market only; inclusions (utilities, parking, appliances) vary. Guidance only.
